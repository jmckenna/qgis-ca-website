Learn about QGIS

QGIS is the most popular open source GIS desktop tool in the world.

Group Goals

  • Promote the use of QGIS in Canada's government agencies, educational institutions, and private sector
  • Provide training and support for new QGIS users in Canada
  • Coordinate local QGIS development and customization for Canadian needs
  • Share information about QGIS through meetings, social media, and local GIS events
  • Support translation of QGIS documentation into French
  • Create specialized modules for local applications (agriculture, urban planning, conservation)
  • Organize regular user meetings across Canada
  • Connect Canadian QGIS users with the global QGIS community
